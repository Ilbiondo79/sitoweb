Solutions Overview

What Humphree brings to commercial boating

Interceptor Systems

Precision blades with fast-acting electric actuators manage running trim, reduce drag, and keep decks level in crosswinds or uneven loads.

Stabilizing Fins

Carbon-fiber fins with compact drives minimize roll at low and zero speeds, improving crew efficiency and passenger comfort.

Active Ride Control

Software-driven coordination of interceptors, fins, and trim tabs adapts to changing sea states for safer high-speed runs.

Coordinated Turn Control

Automatic heeling compensation maintains visibility and deck safety during tight maneuvers for pilot, patrol, and SAR crews.

Stability Augmentation

Pitch and roll damping packages integrate with onboard sensors to protect sensitive equipment and reduce fatigue.

Hybrid & Electric Ready

Lightweight components and smart power management support modern propulsion architectures without compromising endurance.
